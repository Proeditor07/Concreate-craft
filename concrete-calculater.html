    How to calculate the right amount of concrete

    Use our concrete calculator to help give an accurate amount of concrete required for your next project.

    Top Tip: You can build up a concrete calculation for complicated areas by adding additional shapes to the calculation.

    Step 1
    Measure the dimensions (length, width and depth) of the area you want to concrete.

    Step 2
    Double check the measurements, ideally get a second person to remeasure for accuracy.

    Step 3
    Enter these measurements into the calculator and select the correct units of measure.

    Step 4
    Make a note of the total concrete volume needed shown in M3.

    Step 5
    Select the application above to find the perfect concrete mix for your project.

    Step 6
    Select your concrete mix and enter your quantity (m3) to get a price before proceeding to checkout.
